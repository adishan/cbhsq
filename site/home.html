SAMHSA partners with NCHS to make Restricted NSDUH data

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration has partnered with the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS) to host restricted data from the National Survey on Drug Use and Health (NSDUH).

Eligible researchers may now apply for access to these data. For more information please visit the FAQ here.
